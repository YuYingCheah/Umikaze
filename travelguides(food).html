Ichiran Ramen

First time at ICHIRAN

Shop List in Tokyo

Ichiran Ramen is a renowned Japanese restaurant chain known for its personalized dining experience and delicious tonkotsu ramen. Founded in Fukuoka in 1960, it has expanded globally.

Ichiran offers solitary dining booths where customers can customize their ramen's broth richness, noodle firmness, flavor intensity, and toppings. Known for its creamy pork bone broth and attention to detail, Ichiran provides a unique and memorable dining experience for ramen enthusiasts worldwide.

Joto Curry, Shibuya

Japan, 〒150-0002 Tokyo, Shibuya City, Shibuya, 3 Chome−18−7 東一号館ビル １Ｆ

A lunchtime line of salarymen frequently shows the way to a cheap but good meal in Tokyo. We joined the line at Joto Curry yesterday on a whim to see what all the hype was about. With one mouthful, we knew why Joto Curry is such a local favorite.

This is one of the best curries we've eaten in Tokyo, with a thick sauce that's wonderfully spiced and sweet, and juicy katsu with a deliciously crunchy coating. What was the one thing we disliked? Staring at our empty, finished plates, knowing there was nothing more to be had.
