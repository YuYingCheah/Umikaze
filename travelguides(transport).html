Public Bus

How to Ride the Toei Bus

Buses in Tokyo are helpful for their north-south routes in the western side of the city, as trains often travel east-west.

Become overcrowded during rush hours, but there are no pushers. Operating hour: 7:00 a.m. to 23:00 p.m.(Daily), however some routes additionally include night buses. Bus schedule: Every 10 minutes during rush hours, every 15-20 minutes during off-peak hours. Fares: Mostly charge a fixed price of 210 yen (RM6.36) each journey.

Shinkansen (Bullet Train)

Shinkansen tickets: How to buy and use

Japan's high-speed bullet train, the Shinkansen, was established in 1964 and connects major cities such as Tokyo, Osaka, and Kyoto at speeds of up to 320 km/h (200 mph). The Shinkansen is known for its punctuality, efficiency, and safety, and it includes seismic detection and automated braking systems. Its clean design and cosy interiors make it a favourite travel destination.
