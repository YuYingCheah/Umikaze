ABOUT US

For all adventures worldwide.

We think everyone deserves to be allowed to travel. It improves in our understanding of the outside world and ourselves.

Our objectives is to make discovery enjoyable for everyone as possible. Because we think that doing so develops a world that is kinder, more accepting and more tolerant.

We are born travelers, just like you. Travel, in our opinion at Umikaze, is the path to the finest and most memorable experiences life has to offer. And we've found that, as opposed to simply taking in the sights, the best travel experiences involve putting yourself out there, escaping the ordinary, and fully investing oneself.
